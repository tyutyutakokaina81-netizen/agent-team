## What actually matters for streaming

Before naming any tool, here's what genuinely affects your streaming experience:

- **Speed and consistency** — Buffering usually comes from throughput drops, not raw peak speed. A VPN that stays steady matters more than one with a flashy top number.
- **Server count and locations** — More servers in more countries generally means less congestion and more options while traveling.
- **Device and platform support** — Smart TVs, Fire Stick, routers, phones, and consoles all differ. Native apps beat manual workarounds.
- **Reliability of access** — Streaming platforms actively detect and block VPN traffic, so "works today" doesn't guarantee "works next month." No provider can honestly promise permanent access to any specific service.
- **Privacy posture** — A clear no-logs stance (ideally independently audited) and jurisdiction matter if privacy is part of your reason.
- **Price vs. commitment** — Long-term plans are cheaper per month but lock you in. Check the refund window.
