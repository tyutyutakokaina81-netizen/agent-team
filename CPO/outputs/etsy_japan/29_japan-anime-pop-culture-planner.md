- This kit assumes you'll be visiting hobby-and-electronics districts, anime-themed locations, arcades, and merch shops.

### Page 11 — Quick Etiquette & Practical Notes
- Photos: don't photograph staff, customers, or inside venues without permission; follow posted rules.
- Lines: themed venues and limited-goods drops can have long, orderly queues — arrive early.
- Packaging: ask for extra protection for fragile goods; carry a small box or padding.
- Power: arcades and shopping days drain phones fast — carry a battery pack.
- Respect residents near "pilgrimage" spots; keep noise down and don't trespass.

This planner is general guidance only. Always confirm the latest official details for any venue, event, or collaboration before you go.
