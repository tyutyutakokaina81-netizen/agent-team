Page 5 — Rail Basics & Glossary
- Shinkansen: high-speed bullet train network linking major cities.
- Limited Express: fast intercity train, slower than shinkansen, often regional.
- Local / Rapid: short-distance trains, no reservation needed.
- Reserved seat: assigned seat, recommended on busy dates.
- Non-reserved seat: first-come seating in set cars.
- IC card: rechargeable tap card for local trains, subways, and buses.
- Green car: premium carriage with wider seats.
- Tip: Some fastest trains may not be covered by every pass — check before boarding.
