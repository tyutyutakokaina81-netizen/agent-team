Page 6 — Season-by-Season Guide
- Okinawa is subtropical. Pick activities to match your travel month, and check current conditions.
| Season | General highlights | Plan-ahead notes |
|---|---|---|
| Spring | Mild, fewer crowds, early swimming | check water temps |
| Summer | Warm sea, peak beach season | hot & humid; typhoon season risk — watch forecasts |
| Autumn | Warm, gradually calmer | typhoons still possible — check latest |
| Winter | Cooler, quieter, sightseeing-focused | swimming limited; off-peak prices |
- Reminder: typhoons can disrupt ferries and flights in some seasons. Always check latest and keep flexible plans.
