Page 8 — Deer Park: Etiquette & Safety
- The park is home to free-roaming wild deer. They are wild animals — be calm and respectful.
- Guidelines to research and follow:
  - [ ] Buy only the official deer crackers sold on site; don't feed other food
  - [ ] Don't tease or hold food above the deer for long — they may push
  - [ ] Keep bags, maps, and tickets tucked away (deer may grab paper)
  - [ ] Supervise children closely around the animals
  - [ ] Move slowly; avoid sudden movements
  - [ ] Don't touch faces or chase the deer
- Note: park rules and feeding guidance can change; check current local signs and official advice.
