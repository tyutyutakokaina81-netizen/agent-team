Page 13 — Transport & Driving Notes
- Hokkaido is spread out. Many travelers rent a car for flexibility, especially outside cities; trains and buses also connect main towns.

- IC cards / passes: prepaid cards work in cities; check whether any area rail pass fits your plan (compare current prices).
