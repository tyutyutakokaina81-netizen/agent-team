Page 3 — How to Use This Planner
- Step 1: Decide if you have one day or an overnight, then pick the matching plan page.
- Step 2: Check ferry and tram times on the Access page and build your timeline around them.
- Step 3: For Miyajima, glance at the Tide Notes — the famous gate and shrine look very different at high vs low tide.
- Tip: Mornings are quieter on the island; crowds build by midday.

Page 4 — Area Overview
- Hiroshima city: home to the Peace Memorial Park and Museum, a riverside downtown, and a reborn modern city center.
- Miyajima Island: a short ferry ride away, famous for its floating-style shrine gate, forested hills, and free-roaming deer.
- Getting around the city: trams (streetcars) and buses cover most sights.
- Typical split: half a day for the Peace Memorial area, half to a full day for Miyajima.
