# Quick-Start Examples — Run These On Day One

Don't read the whole system and freeze. Activate one officer and run a real
task. Here are five to start with.

---

### 1. Marketing (CMO) — get your first asset
> Activate the CMO. "Here's my product: [one line]. My customer is [who].
> Write a landing-page headline and three subheadlines, and tell me which one
> to test first and why."

### 2. Product (CPO) — sharpen what you sell
> Activate the CPO. "My product is [X]. List the single outcome a buyer wants,
> then the 3 things the product must do to deliver it. Cut anything that
> doesn't."

### 3. Finance (CFO) — pressure-test your price
> Activate the CFO. "I want to charge [price] for [product]. Show me what I'd
> earn at 10, 50, and 200 sales a month, and tell me the biggest risk to those
> numbers."

### 4. Analytics (CAO) — find the real lever
> Activate the CAO. "Here's my situation: [traffic, sales, whatever you have].
> What's the one number I should try to move first, and how would I test it?"

### 5. Two officers — your first project
> "Project: launch [product] next month. CMO plans the campaign; CPO confirms
> the product is ready. Hand off through a project brief and give me one
> combined plan."

---

## The habit that makes it stick

After each task, have the officer write one line to its `_log.md`:

```
| 2026-01-15 | landing headline v1 | marketing | 3 options, testing #1 | done |
```

Do that for two weeks and your AI company will know more about your business
than most assistants you could hire. That's the whole game: **structure plus
memory, run consistently.**
