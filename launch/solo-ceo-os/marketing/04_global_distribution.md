# Global Distribution Playbook — selling to the world

The product, the price ($19 USD), and the checkout (Gumroad handles global tax
as merchant of record) are already world-ready. What's left is **discovery**:
where on Earth do buyers of digital/AI products actually find things?

Ordered by leverage. Don't do all at once — work top to bottom.

---

## Tier 1 — Big global launch moments (do these first)

### 1. Product Hunt
The single biggest one-day spike of global early-adopters for digital tools.
- **First action:** create a maker account now; "tease" the product; schedule a
  launch for a Tue–Thu.
- **What to post:** the product, the Blueprint as a free taster, a 60-sec
  story ("I run my business with 6 AI executives").
- **Reach:** a decent launch = thousands of global visitors in 24h.

### 2. Hacker News (Show HN)
Reaches a worldwide founder/builder audience that buys $19 tools without
thinking.
- **First action:** post "Show HN: I run my one-person company with 6 AI
  executives — here's the system."
- **Rule:** be humble, link the free blueprint, answer every comment. HN
  punishes hype, rewards substance — which is your strength (it's real).

### 3. Indie Hackers
Founders building solo businesses = your exact buyer, globally.
- **First action:** write a post about the *system* (not an ad): "How I
  structured my AI like a company." Link blueprint at the end.

---

## Tier 2 — Always-on global discovery (set up once, runs forever)

### 4. Gumroad Discover
Gumroad's own marketplace surfaces products to global buyers searching for
templates/AI tools — **free traffic if set up right.**
- **First action:** in the product, add strong tags (ai, productivity,
  templates, solopreneur, prompts, notion), a keyword-rich title, and enable
  Discover. Gumroad takes a higher cut on Discover sales, but it's traffic you
  didn't pay for.

### 5. SEO landing page
People worldwide search "AI prompts for business," "run business with AI,"
"AI assistant roles." Your page can rank.
- **First action:** make sure the page `<title>` and headings include those
  phrases (already done), and publish 2–3 supporting blog posts that link to it.
- **Slow but permanent** — compounds for years with zero ongoing cost.

### 6. YouTube / TikTok (short demo)
A 60-second screen recording "watch my AI act as 6 executives" travels globally
and is evergreen.
- **First action:** one screen-capture demo, posted to both. Pin the blueprint
  link.

---

## Tier 3 — Niche global communities (steady trickle)

- **Reddit (worldwide):** r/Entrepreneur, r/SideProject, r/Solopreneur,
  r/ChatGPT, r/ArtificialIntelligence — value post + blueprint in first comment.
- **Twitter/X:** the thread from `03_launch_posts.md`, re-run weekly with new
  angles. Reply to people complaining about messy AI workflows.
- **Discord/Slack communities** for indie founders and AI builders.

---

## The 3-week global launch sequence

| Week | Focus | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 1** | Foundation | Gumroad live + Discover on, page deployed, email funnel armed, X thread posted |
| **Week 2** | Communities | Reddit + Indie Hackers + LinkedIn posts; collect first feedback/testimonials |
| **Week 3** | Big moment | Product Hunt + Show HN on the same good week; ride the spike |

After Week 3, the always-on channels (Discover + SEO + weekly X) keep traffic
trickling while you repeat the best-performing posts.

---

## Why this product fits a global audience specifically

- **No localization needed** — the system is language-agnostic; buyers run it in
  their own language.
- **Universal pain** — "AI gives shallow output" is felt by solopreneurs in
  every country.
- **A real, unusual story** — "I run my business with AI executives" is
  share-worthy worldwide, not a generic template.
- **Frictionless global checkout** — $19, instant download, Gumroad handles
  every country's tax. Anyone on Earth can buy in 30 seconds.
