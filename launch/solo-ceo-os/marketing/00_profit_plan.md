# Solo CEO OS — Profit Plan (the number, and the math behind it)

This is the honest target for **this one product**. Not the whole company —
just Solo CEO OS. No hope, just funnel math.

---

## The unit economics

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Price | $19 (≈ ¥2,850) |
| Gumroad + payment fees | ~10% (≈ ¥300) |
| **Net profit per sale** | **≈ ¥2,500** |
| Fixed monthly cost | ¥5,800 (Claude + tools) |

Every sale after the **third** each month is pure profit.

## The funnel (conservative assumptions)

```
Visitors → 10% join email list → 3% of list buy over time
Visitors → 1.5% buy directly
```

So roughly: **100 visitors ≈ 2–3 sales ≈ ¥5,000–7,500.**

## The profit target — staged, tied to reality

| Phase | When | Sales/mo | Revenue | What it requires |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **0. Break-even** | Month 1 | 3 | ¥7.5K | First launch posts + the email funnel live |
| **1. Proof** | Month 2–3 | 12 | ¥30K | ~500 visitors/mo from content; list ~400 |
| **2. Traction** | Month 4–6 | 32 | ¥80K | Steady weekly content; list ~1,000 |
| **3. Real income** | Month 6–9 | 60 | ¥150K | Audience + **price ladder** (below) |

**The headline target: ¥80K/month profit from this product by Month 6,
on the path to ¥150K by Month 9.**

This product alone realistically tops out around ¥100–150K/month. The
company-wide ¥300K goal comes from **stacking** this with the Gumroad templates
and Substack — not from this one product carrying everything. (That was the old
mistake.)

## How to raise income without more traffic — the price ladder

More traffic is slow. Raising the average sale is fast. Add tiers later:

| Tier | Price | What's added |
|---|---|---|
| Core (now) | $19 | The system (this package) |
| Plus | $49 | + filled-in examples, a Notion version, video walkthrough |
| Pro | $149 | + a 1:1 setup call (your time, high margin) |

If 1 in 5 buyers takes Plus and 1 in 50 takes Pro, the **average sale roughly
doubles** — same visitors, double the profit. This is the real lever once the
funnel works.

## Who owns what (the AI company runs this)

- **CMO** — drives all traffic: content, launch posts, the email sequence.
- **CAO** — watches the numbers monthly: visitors, signup %, conversion %; says
  what to change.
- **CPO** — builds the Plus/Pro tiers when Phase 2 hits.
- **CFO** — confirms the unit economics as price/fees change.
- **You** — approve, and do the few manual steps tools can't (post, deploy).

The target is a number with a path, not a wish. Phase 0 (break-even) is the
only one that matters this month. Hit 3 sales, and the rest is iteration.
