## Email 3 — Day 3 · The missing piece (memory)

**memory** — each officer keeping a short log of what it did, and reading it
before the next task.

Without it, you re-explain your business every session. With it, your AI knows
more about your company every week.

It's a tiny habit — one logged line per task — and it's the difference between
an assistant and a team. The full system bakes this in so you don't have to
think about it.
