## Description (body)

### You're not disorganized. You're understaffed.

Running solo means being the marketer, the product person, the finance person, the
salesperson, and the ops person — all at once, all badly, all day. Your AI assistant
should help, but "hey ChatGPT, help with my business" gives you a smart intern with
no memory and no lane.

**Solo CEO OS fixes that.** It installs six AI executives into the assistant you
already use. Each has a real job, a point of view, and a shared rulebook — so you
stop chatting with a bot and start delegating to a team.

### Meet your executive team

- **CMO** — writes your launches, posts, and pages for reach and emotion.
- **CPO** — turns your messy knowledge into products people can actually finish.
- **CFO** — refuses to be vague about pricing, numbers, and risk.
- **CSO** — handles customer objections and proposals without losing trust.
- **CDO** — automates the boring parts and even hires new executives when you grow.
- **CAO** — tells you *why* last month worked, so you repeat it on purpose.

They each stay in their lane. They disagree on purpose — exactly like a real
leadership team. You make the calls. You're the CEO.

### It remembers your business

The killer feature: a **persistent memory file** your AI reads at the start of every
session. Fill it in once and you never re-explain your business again. Decisions,
current projects, next steps — all carried forward, across days and even across tools.

### It grows with you

Hit a task no one owns? Tell the CDO. If it fits the rules, it spins up a new
executive — HR, Legal, Customer Experience — with its own workspace. Your org chart
grows to match your business.

### What you get

- The complete Solo CEO OS folder (README + 30-minute Quickstart)
- Six ready-to-use executive role prompts
- The shared company rulebook (`company.md`)
- Drop-in instructions for Claude Code, Claude Desktop, ChatGPT, and Cursor
- The persistent-memory template that makes it all compound
- Free updates within v1.x

### Runs on what you already have

No new subscription. No code. If your AI assistant can read a folder of files —
Claude or ChatGPT, desktop or web — you're ready in 30 minutes.

### Honest expectations

This is an operating system, not a magic money button. It won't run your business
for you — it gives your business a structure and a team so *you* run it far better.
Built and used daily by a real solo operator.
