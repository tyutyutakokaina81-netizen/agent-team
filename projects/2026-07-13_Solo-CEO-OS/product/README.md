# Solo CEO OS

**Run your one-person business like a company — with a team of AI executives.**

You are the CEO. This OS gives you six C-level executives (Marketing, Product,
Finance, Sales, Tech, Analytics) that live inside your AI assistant. Each one has
a defined role, a point of view, its own workspace, and a shared rulebook — so
instead of asking a blank chatbot "help me with my business," you delegate to the
right executive and get work that fits together.

It runs on any capable AI assistant that can read a folder of files
(Claude Code, Claude Desktop with a project, ChatGPT projects, Cursor, etc.).
No code, no subscriptions beyond the AI you already use.

## The idea in one minute

A solo founder wears every hat badly at once. This OS separates the hats.

- **Each executive has a lane.** The CMO writes for emotion and reach. The CFO
  refuses to be vague about numbers. The CPO only cares whether the customer
  reaches the goal. They disagree on purpose — that's what a real exec team does.
- **They share one rulebook** (`company.md`): how to report, where files live,
  how to separate fact from opinion, how work moves between roles.
- **They remember.** `STATE.md` is a living memory file your AI reads at the start
  of every session, so you never re-explain your business.
- **They can hire.** When a task fits no existing role and will recur, the CDO
  spins up a new executive (CHO for hiring, CLO for legal, etc.) on a fixed template.

## How you actually use it

1. Put this folder where your AI can read it (a Claude Code repo, a Claude/ChatGPT
   project, a Cursor workspace).
2. Fill in `memory/STATE.template.md` with your business in 10 minutes.
3. Talk to an executive by name: *"CMO, draft this week's launch email."*
   *"CFO, is $19 the right price for this?"* *"CAO, why did last month underperform?"*
4. At the end of a session, tell your AI to update `STATE.md`. Next time it picks
   up exactly where you left off.

Full walk-through in **QUICKSTART.md**.

## Who this is for

- Solo founders, freelancers, and creators who feel like a one-person overwhelm.
- People already using an AI assistant daily who want structure, not another chatbot.
- Anyone who has thought "I wish I had a team" but can't (or won't) hire yet.

## Who this is NOT for

- Teams that already have real department heads.
- People looking for a done-for-you agency. This is an operating system you drive.
