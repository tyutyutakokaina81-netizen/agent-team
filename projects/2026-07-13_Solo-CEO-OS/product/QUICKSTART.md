# QUICKSTART — running in 30 minutes

You need: an AI assistant that can read a folder of files. Any of these work —
**Claude Code**, **Claude Desktop** (with a Project), **ChatGPT** (with a Project),
or **Cursor**. That's the only requirement.

---

## Step 1 — Put the OS where your AI can see it (5 min)

**Claude Code / Cursor:** copy this whole folder into your working directory. The
`CLAUDE.md` file is picked up automatically.

**Claude Desktop / ChatGPT Projects:** create a new Project. Upload `company.md`,
the six files in `roles/`, and your filled-in `STATE.md`. Paste the "Operating
instructions" section of `CLAUDE.md` into the Project's custom instructions.

---

## Step 2 — Make it *your* company (10 min)

1. Copy `memory/STATE.template.md` to `memory/STATE.md`.
2. Fill in the blanks: what your business is, who the customer is, what you're
   selling, what you're working on right now. Be honest and specific — vague input
   gives vague executives.
3. Optional but powerful: drop a few notes into `context/` (a `diary/` entry about
   how this week went, an `ideas/` file with what you want to try). Every executive
   reads `context/` before working.

---

## Step 3 — Meet your executives (5 min)

Address a role by name and watch it stay in its lane:

> **"CMO, give me three launch-post angles for my new template."**
> **"CFO, I'm about to price a service at $50/mo — poke holes in that."**
> **"CPO, turn my messy notes into a 5-step guide a beginner can follow."**
> **"CSO, a lead said 'it's too expensive.' Draft a reply that keeps trust."**
> **"CAO, here are last month's numbers — what's the one thing I should change?"**

If you don't name a role, your AI picks the right one and tells you which.

---

## Step 4 — The habit that makes it compound (5 min to learn, seconds to run)

At the end of any working session, say:

> **"Update STATE.md with what we decided and what's next."**

Your AI writes the current state, the decisions, and the next actions into
`STATE.md`. Next session, it reads that first — so you never re-explain your
business, and the company remembers across days, weeks, and tools.

---

## Optional — hire a new executive

When you keep hitting a task none of the six own (say, hiring, or legal), tell the CDO:

> **"CDO, I keep needing HR work. Should we add a role?"**

If it fits the rules in `company.md` §5, the CDO creates a CHO with its own folder
and prompt, and updates the org chart. Your company grows to fit your business.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **Executives feel generic** → your `STATE.md` and `context/` are thin. Add detail.
- **They forget things** → you skipped Step 4. Update `STATE.md` at the end of sessions.
- **Two executives disagree** → that's the design. Ask the CAO to break the tie with
  a hypothesis, or make the call yourself. You're the CEO.

That's it. You're running a company of one — as a company.
