## Product description (no hype)

If you run a business alone — as a founder, freelancer, or indie worker — AI is the closest thing you have to a team. But most "AI prompt" collections are either vague, bloated, or written for marketers selling courses.

This is a working pack. 28 prompts you copy, fill in a few brackets, and use the same day, organized by the actual jobs a solo operator has to do: planning, research, writing, admin groundwork, customer and sales messages, and your own focus and decisions.

Each prompt comes with a plain "use it when" line so you find the right one fast, and a practical tip for getting a better result than the first draft. Several prompts are built specifically to keep AI honest — to stop it inventing facts, overclaiming, or producing generic copy with your name on it.

It also includes a reusable "context block" that makes every prompt sharper, a self-review prompt to run on AI's own output before you ship it, and an honest list of the things you should *not* hand to an AI.

No fluff, no padding, no theory you have to read through to reach the useful part. If you already use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or a similar assistant, this gives you a tested starting point for the work you actually do.
