## 使い方（3ステップ）
1. 下の **Tier1（投稿しやすい）** から2〜3媒体を選ぶ
2. 各サイトの "Write for us / Pitch / Contact" を開いて**現行規定を確認**
3. 末尾の **英語ピッチ雛形**を媒体別にカスタムして送る（送付実行は cowork/オーナー）

## 英語ピッチ雛形（媒体別に[ ]を差し替え）

```
Subject: Pitch — The real hometown of Doraemon: a quiet day-trip from Kanazawa

Dear [Editor name / Editors],

Most travelers based in Kanazawa spend day two on the crowded reserved bus to
Shirakawa-go. Fifteen minutes away by Shinkansen is Takaoka — the town where
Fujiko F. Fujio, the creator of Doraemon, actually grew up. I live nearby, and
on a normal weekend the quiet surprise isn't the statues in the park; it's the
line of foreign visitors waiting their turn to photograph them.

I'd like to write a ~1,200-word first-person essay on this overlooked, calmer
side of Japan — not anime tourism, but a thoughtful cultural detour that can end
in Gokayama, a World Heritage gassho village far quieter than Shirakawa-go.

I'm a Takaoka resident writing in English for overseas readers. [one-line bio /
link]. Would this fit [section name]? Happy to send a full draft on spec.

Best regards,
[Name]
```

## 安全・品質メモ
- 公開連絡先のみ使用。個人の私的住所/PIIは扱わない（媒体の公式窓口は可）。
- 「世界唯一」等の誇張は雛形に入れない（A5）。事実ベース。
- 各媒体の現行規定は**送付前に都度確認**（本リストは時点情報）。
