## Reddit r/JapanTravel（リード1本・宣伝臭NG/正直レビュー調）
**Title:** If you're spending 2 days in Kanazawa, consider Takaoka (Doraemon's real hometown) instead of only Shirakawa-go
**Body:**
Most itineraries here send people to Shirakawa-go on day 2 (great, but crowded + a reserved 75-min bus). Less known: Takaoka is 15 min from Kanazawa by Shinkansen (no reservation), and it's where Fujiko F. Fujio — the creator of Doraemon — actually grew up. Free park with the character statues, a Doraemon tram, a statue street by the station, and you can ride on to Gokayama (quieter than Shirakawa-go). Easy half-day. Full write-up: <URL>. Happy to answer questions.
