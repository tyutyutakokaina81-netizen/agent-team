## 4. Measurement & the two exits

Track the funnel: **Pinterest impressions → outbound clicks → English page → exit (affiliate / e-book).**

**Pinterest Analytics (free, weekly check):**
- **Impressions & saves** per pin — which titles/images earn saves (saves = long-tail distribution; a saved pin keeps working for months).
- **Outbound clicks** per pin — the metric that matters most (clicks reach the site). Rank pins by outbound clicks, not impressions.
- **Top boards** — which board drives the most clicks; double down on that theme.
- Note **which keyword angle wins** (off-the-beaten-path vs food vs Alps) and lean the next batch that way.

**On-site (English page → exit):**
- Confirm each `en-*.html` page has both exits visible: **affiliate links** (`affiliates.js`) and the **e-book** CTA. A high-click pin that lands on a page with no exit wastes the traffic.
- Watch which landing pages convert to exit clicks (page-level referral from Pinterest). Food and itinerary pages tend to convert differently than scenery pages — record which.

**Reporting loop:**
- Monthly, log to `ops/outbox/` for code → CAO: top 5 pins by outbound clicks, top 5 by saves, best board, best keyword angle, and any page that got clicks but no exit conversions (fix candidate).
- Kill/refresh the bottom pins: pins with high impressions but near-zero clicks get a new title/image; pins with zero impressions after 4 weeks get re-pinned to a different board.

## 5. Guardrails (check before every upload)

1. **Real photos only (A2/A3):** owner shots or verified commercial-license stock (Unsplash/Pexels). No AI images, no re-uploading others' Pinterest images. Firefly-squid and snow-wall photos especially — verify the image genuinely shows the subject.
2. **No exaggeration (A5):** keep "one of / among / a local's" hedges. No "world's only" / "Japan's #1" without basis. Prices, seasons and train times are always "roughly / can change."
3. **No PII / location detail (A4):** city-level only. No districts, addresses, private property, or identifiable faces in photos.
4. **Existing URLs only:** every target URL is an `en-*.html` page that exists in `apps/toyama-guide/`. If a new page is added, confirm it's live before pinning.
5. **No spam pattern:** don't post the same pin twice; don't dump a page's 3 pins in one week; keep to ~4–5 fresh pins/week.
