### Title
> The "Water Mirror" season has arrived in Toyama, Japan.

### Body
> Right now, the rice paddies in my hometown are flooded for the planting season. For just a few weeks, the entire landscape turns into a giant mirror.
>
> In this photo, you can see the Tateyama Mountain Range (still capped with snow) reflected perfectly on the water's surface. We call this "Mizu-kagami" (Water Mirror). It feels as if the mountains have descended from the sky to rest on the earth.
>
> It's a quiet, busy time for the farmers, but for us locals, it's a moment to stop and realize that we live in a masterpiece of nature.
>
> Does your region have a specific seasonal "miracle" like this?
